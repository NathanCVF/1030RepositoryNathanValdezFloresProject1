Attentional Deficiancies, Possible Causes, and Solutions

This website gives statistical facts about how many individuals suffer from attention deficiancy, and how many people go without proper diagnosis and treatment.

The website gives the user a set of listed links and websties that can take them to platforms that can diagnose whether they have deficiancy, the causes of it, and offer treatments/solutions to the problems.

The site also lets the user take a minature Stroop Test in order to better understand how strong their attention is as of now. The purpose of the website is to give easier access and directions for users to gain assistance for attention issues.

Information about attention issues in the coming years

  1. About 11.4% of adolecents Are diagnosed with ADHD
  2. About 4-5% of adults Are diagnosed with ADHD
  3. 78% of diagnosed adolecents have other condidtions that contribute to attentional issues
  4. Most adults were diagnosed with ADHD and attention issues later in their lives
  5. 30% of adults with anxiety and depression have difficulty with attention
  6. 40% of adults with attention issues also have poor sleeping habits
  7. Over half of online users suffer attention decline from excessive screentime
  8. Over 10% of youths who use substances end up with attention issues
